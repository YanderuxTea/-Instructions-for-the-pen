Instruction manual.

If you are a beginner, this guide will help you use a smart pen that can take photos and make calls. The pen looks like an ordinary writing instrument, but it is equipped with a miniature camera, microphone and Bluetooth.

  • The beginning. Press and hold the main button for three seconds until the LED turns blue. This means that the handle is on.
  • To take a picture, point the pen clip at the subject and press the button twice. The pen vibrates slightly, confirming that the picture has been taken. All photos are automatically saved in the accompanying app, which can be downloaded to your smartphone via Google Play or the App Store.
  • To make a call, open the app, connect the pen via Bluetooth and select "Ringer Mode". Once connected, you can call any contact from your phone while simultaneously talking and listening through the pen. The built-in microphone works best if you hold the pen next to your mouth.
  • Do not forget to charge the pen with a magnetic cable for at least an hour before using it for the first time.
